Interesting facts about otters

  • Otters are adept swimmers found in waterways on every continent except Australia and Antarctica

  • They have incredibly thick fur that traps air, making them buoyant and well-insulated

  • There are 13 species of otters worldwide, and many are currently endangered

  • Otters have webbed feet and can hold their breath underwater for over four minutes, showcasing their aquatic prowess
